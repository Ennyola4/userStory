INTRODUCTION

Purpose

This document outlines the business requirements for deploying the Business Banking Service across Alert Group. Rather than prescribing technical solutions, it serves as a strategic guide to:

  1. Define the core business functionality
  2. Establish clear operational parameters
  3. Align cross-functional teams on service objectives
  4. An instruction to all project team and implementing vendors on what to build.
  5. A baseline to control change
  6. A point of reference for the project team for the entire project duration with respect to requirements.

Business Structure

Digital Banking: A Strategic Priority for Alert Group

Digital banking represents a transformative opportunity for Alert Group, offering:
  • Enhanced customer experiences through seamless digital interactions
  • Cost savings by migrating existing customers’ transactions away from branches to more efficient electronic channels
  • New revenue opportunities through servicing third parties, new customer segments (MSMEs’, SMEs’, youth, unbanked) and convenience-driven growth in service uptake by existing customers.
  • Operational efficiencies from streamlined processes and automation
  • Competitive differentiation in an increasingly digital financial landscape
  • Send money: Bill payments, merchant payments, fund transfers, salary administration, supplier & vendor payments.
  • Digital channels: Request POS, Card.
  • Receive: Collections, account deposits

Our executions should meet the following customer expectations

  • Cost effective option to cash.
  • Reliability – Instant transactions, certainty of transactions, traceable
  • New revenue potential via innovative products and services
  • Wide distribution – Business owners, merchants, remote access to products and services.

Overview of Product

Vision of Product

Alert Business Banking is a sophisticated yet intuitive web-based platform designed to streamline digital payments for businesses of all sizes. Our solution empowers organizations to manage their financial operations with unmatched ease and security.

  • Comprehensive Features: Combines single & bulk transfers, bill payments, analytics, and budgeting in one app.
  • Anytime, Anywhere Access: Cloud-based platform available 24/7 from any device
  • Enterprise-Grade Security: Bank-level encryption and compliance protocols
  • User-Centric Design: Simplified workflows with customizable preferences, User management
  • Enhanced Security: Biometric login, OTP validation, and 2FA for sensitive transactions
  • Seamless Integration: Works with your existing financial workflows
  • Scalability: Designed to support millions of users and integrate future financial services seamlessly.
  • Analytics-Driven: Advanced tools for expense tracking, budgeting, and financial insights.

Target Market

Growth-focused businesses that value:

  • Digital transformation
  • Financial control

  • Payment efficiency

Competitive Advantages

  3. Business-First Features: Tailored tools for corporate financial management

    • Summary page contains the ffg details - Transfer Amount, Transfer fee (if applicable), Total debit amount, Beneficiary name, Account number and Bank name.

    Enters 4-digit PIN.

    NOTE

    • System processes payment and returns “Transfer Successful!” OR “Failed” if unsuccessful.
    • User has the option to ’Add beneficiary”, “Share receipt”, “Download receipt”, OR “Go to Dashboard”
    • User receives SMS and email notification on transaction status
  • Selects “Proceed to Dashboard” to navigate to the Home Page

    • Additional Notes: (Validation Scenarios)

    • User should not be able to proceed to the next step, if the beneficiary’s name is not validated.
    • Account number field must be numeric and not allow more than 11 characters.
    • Transfer amount must be numeric and not exceed more than 2 decimal points.
    • Where User attempts to transfer more than the available account balance (with the transaction fee in consideration), system should display: “Insufficient funds” at the page before submitting the request
    • Where transfer amount exceeds either the single or daily transfer limit, system prompts “Singe Transaction Limit exceeded” or “Daily Transaction limit exceeded” as the case maybe. Before submitting the request.
    • If the account is inactive or blocked, system displays: “Transaction not allowed. Please contact customer support.”
